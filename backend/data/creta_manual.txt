8. Emergency Situations

Hazard warning flasher
In case of an emergency whilst driving, turn on the hazard warning flasher to warn other road users.

If the engine stalls whilst driving
Reduce speed gradually, pull off the road safely, and switch on hazard warning lights.

If the engine stalls at a crossroad or crossing
If possible, move the vehicle out of traffic. If not, turn on hazard warning lights and seek assistance.

If you have a flat tyre whilst driving
Reduce speed slowly, hold the steering wheel firmly, and pull over to a safe location.

If the engine does not start
Check the battery connections. If required, jump-start the vehicle using proper procedures.

Jump starting
Follow the correct jump-starting sequence to avoid damage or injury.

Overheating
Stop the vehicle immediately if the engine overheats and allow it to cool before checking coolant levels.
